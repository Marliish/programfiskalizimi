## 🎨 Design Philosophy Notes

### Our Differentiation from devPOS
1. **More modern UI** - They look dated, we're 2026 modern
2. **Better typography** - Inter > their font choice
3. **Touch-first** - They're mouse-first, we're touch-optimized
4. **Accessibility** - We're WCAG AA, they're not
5. **Professional colors** - Our blue is trustworthy, theirs is generic
6. **Component consistency** - We have design system, they don't seem to

### Key Design Decisions Rationale
- **Blue primary:** Trust, professionalism, calm (finance industry standard)
- **Inter font:** Designed for screens, excellent readability, professional
- **8px grid:** Standard, ensures consistency, easy math
- **48px touch targets:** Faster than 44px, less mistakes for cashiers
- **Split-screen login:** Modern, premium feel, stands out from competition
- **Comprehensive specs:** Developers don't guess = perfect implementation
