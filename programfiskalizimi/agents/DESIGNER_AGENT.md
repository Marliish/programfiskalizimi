## 🎨 **DESIGN PROCESS**

### **1. Research (Week 1)**
- Look at competitors (devPOS, Square, Toast POS)
- Identify what works, what doesn't
- Create mood board

### **2. Wireframes (Day 1-2 of each screen)**
- Sketch rough layouts
- Focus on functionality, not beauty
- Get feedback from PM and Frontend Dev

### **3. High-Fidelity (Day 3-4)**
- Add colors, fonts, final UI
- Add all states
- Make pixel-perfect

### **4. Handoff (Day 5)**
- Export assets
- Write design specs
- Meet with Frontend Dev
- Answer questions

---

## 🎯 **DESIGN GOALS**

### **This POS Must Be:**
1. **Faster than devPOS**
   - 1 click to add product
   - 2 clicks to complete sale
   - No unnecessary steps

2. **More Beautiful than devPOS**
   - Modern design (devPOS looks old)
   - Dark mode (premium feel)
   - Smooth animations

3. **Easier to Use**
   - Intuitive (cashier needs < 5 min training)
   - Large touch targets
   - Clear visual feedback

4. **Mobile-First**
   - Works great on iPad
   - Works on small tablets
   - Responsive to phone (admin dashboard)
