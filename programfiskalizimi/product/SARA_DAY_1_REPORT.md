## 💡 KEY INSIGHTS FROM TODAY

### What I Learned:
1. **Scope is the enemy:** With 500+ features identified, staying focused on MVP is critical. Created clear boundaries (MVP vs Phase 2).

2. **Authentication is foundational:** Sprint 1 is heavy but necessary - everything depends on solid auth and user management.

3. **Offline mode is complex:** This is a key differentiator but technically challenging. Need to monitor closely in Sprint 4.

4. **Fiscalization is the wild card:** Legal compliance can't be skipped, but certification process is uncertain. Must apply early.

5. **Mobile-first is our superpower:** Competitors are web-only or Android-only. iOS + Android with offline gives us a huge advantage.

### Product Strategy Decisions Made:
- ✅ Single location for MVP (defer multi-location to Phase 2)
- ✅ Basic inventory only (defer purchase orders to Phase 2)
- ✅ No restaurant module in MVP (separate it for Phase 2)
- ✅ CRM in MVP (differentiator, simple to build)
- ✅ 13-week timeline is aggressive but achievable
